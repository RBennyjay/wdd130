Temples

Temples are sacred spaces where members of the Church participate in ordinances, make covenants with
God, and find peace through worship and reflection.

The Chicago Illinois Temple

The Chicago Illinois Temple, located in the suburb of Glenview, Illinois, was dedicated in 1985 and serves
members across the Midwest, providing a place for worship, spiritual reflection, and sacred ordinances.
